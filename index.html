About me

I grew up in the Portland, OR area before going abroad to teach English in South Korea. I lived in Sacramento, CA for a few years and then left agin for Bengaluru, India. From India I moved to Bangkok, Thailand before returning to Portland, OR in 2023. Over the last few years I've been pursuing a lifelong interest in computer science by taking courses at Portland State University.

  • Portland State University

    Graduate Preparation Track : Completed Spring 2025

    This program consisted of the core Computer Science courses necessary to be successful in the Master's program at Portland State University.

  • Portland State University

    Bachelor of Arts: Art Practices : August, 2014

    I completed my Art Practices degree with a focus on printmaking.

  • I completed the graduate preparation track in pursuit of a Master of Science in Computer Science degree at Portland State University.

    I worked in a sales role utilizing Oracle Netsuite and Google Workspace.

    In this position I worked closely with clients on the East Coast of the United States selling tile from distributors and manufacturers. I worked directly with these companies and manufacturers who were located around the world - Japan, China, Brazil, Italy, Spain, Portugal, and more. My primary responsibility was utilizing data and spreadsheets to create sales proposals, communicating with shipping companies to incorporate shipping prices and timelines into these proposals, and working with clients in the design phase to offer products that matched their needs and timelines.

    I worked concessions at events for Portland'5 Center for the Arts at the Arlene Schnitzer Concert Hall and the Keller Auditorium.

  • I taught English as a Second Language in Asia.

    After getting my bachelor's degree I got a job teaching English in South Korea. I had a great experience living abroad and so I was motiviated to continue and work in other countries. After South Korea I lived in India and Thailand. There are some photos in my gallery of my time abroad.
